About Bea Taylor Searle

Bio

I am a multidisciplinary artist working between the digital and physical space through sculptures, performative websites and text-based art. The creative process of dissecting the browser allows me to critically investigate our relationship to it. I explore the cursor as an extension of the body and how the internet has become part of our domestic space. My research focuses on how the viewer interacts with the space in the browser and if this space makes the viewer a user.
